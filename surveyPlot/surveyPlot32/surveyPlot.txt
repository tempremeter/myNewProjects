                                           SYRVEYPLOT
                                           Created by
                                         George A Butler

 This product (compiled with C++11, utilizing DEVC++ on MS-Windows 10) is intended for Surveyors, Engineers, Architects, Planners and and Draugthsmem or any other
 person engaged in the preparation of Site plans. It can be utilized to reproduce a Survey plan from the 
 information provided on a legal Survey plan.

 The data is verified by typing the information into a text file. The informatoion is later read into the
 programme and converted to a dxf file for opening in a CAD based program which displays the drawing of 
 the surveyed property. It is a free distribution.

 Your comments on the product will be greatly appreciated.
